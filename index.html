Who We Are

Your World,
Curated.

Ashana Journeys is a registered brand, EST. 2026. We are a Trip Host platform focused on bringing people together through curated group travel experiences. We host travel, hikes, diving, and surfing trips across local and international destinations for those who want more than just travel, real experiences, real connections, and unforgettable moments. We also organize relaxed and light travel experiences for families, beginners, and those who prefer a slower pace. These trips focus on scenic destinations, cultural attractions, temples, and easy going itineraries that anyone can enjoy comfortably. Every trip is carefully planned and run with experienced guides and trusted partners, so you can feel safe, supported, and fully present in the journey with like minded people. Whether you are coming alone, with friends, or with family, you are always welcome here. You are not just joining a trip, you are joining a community.
